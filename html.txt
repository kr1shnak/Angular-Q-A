3. local storage and session storage
A. local storage data will be there even though close browser
	syntax:- localStorage.getItem('name') and localStorage.setItem('name',"content")
	sessionStorage:- when we close the browser data will be lost
	syntax:- sessionStorage.getItem('name') and sessionStorage.setItem('name',"content")

8. Box model
	Content - The content of the box, where text and images appear

	Border - A border that goes around the padding and content
